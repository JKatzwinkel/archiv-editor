Searching for a reference or an aspect

You can also search for aspects or references that contain the word "‘Rome"’. Under “Criteria for Tree”, choose either “Aspect” or “References” in the text field “Search For:”. Click once again on the search button. The result can be found under a new tab in the tree-view.
