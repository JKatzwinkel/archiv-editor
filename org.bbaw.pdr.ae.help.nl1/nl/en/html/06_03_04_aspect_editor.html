Aspect-editor

As explained in chapter ??, aspects are the primary source of information in the Archiv-Editor. The aspect-editor is thus the main tool for entering data. Just as with the person-editor and reference-editor, the aspect-editor is used to create a new aspect or to edit an already existing aspect. The aspect-editor offers however additional possibilities. Besides being able to add textual information, it is also possible to connect to corresponding references, establish markup, define a classification for time and place, as well as to establish relationships to other objects. Administrative data, such as the PDR-ID, the creator of the aspect, and the creation date are also shown in the aspect-editor.

To open the aspect-editor, click ▹Aspect▹NewAspect. If the aspect already exists, then simply double click on it in the aspect-view of the viewing area. Alternatively, the aspect can be clicked on once (thus marking it) and then opened in the aspect-editor by clicking ▹Aspect▹ModifyAspect....
