The main perspective

The main perspective offers a general display of object data in the Archiv-Editor. For this reason it is the standard perspective displayed when the Editor is opened, and in the following chapters it will be the perspective that is used in the examples. The main perspective displays aspects of objects, like persons or references, which can then be grouped and sorted in the aspect-view.

In the next section, special functions of the aspect and tree-view will be explained, such as how to change the sorting criteria for aspects and how to open multiple persons in different tabs. The pictures used to explain these functions show them opened in the main perspective, but these functions can also be used in the two other perspectives (identifier perspective and concurrence perspective).
