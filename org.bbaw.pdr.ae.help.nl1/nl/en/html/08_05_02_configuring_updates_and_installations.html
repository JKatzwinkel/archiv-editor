Configuring updates and installations

Automatic updates and installations (for example from plug-ins) can be administrated under ▹Preferences▹Preferences▹Install/Update. Here and in the subsections “Automatic Updates” and “Available Software Sites”, you can find options for updates, installations, and the removal of program parts. Besides being able to deactivate automatic updates and determine the address where the updated software should be obtained, you can also click “Uninstall or update (Software that is already installed)” and administrate specific parts of the Archiv-Editor program.

You can also update manually by simply clicking ▹Help ▹Update Software in the task menu.
