Faceted search

With a faceted search is possible to search according to specific markup.

If you are looking for the mention of a specific language in the database, for instance, persons who published in that language or spoke that language, select “Faceted: Persons” in the “Search For:” field. A window with a classification schema will now open. Choose the schema “Language” by marking it and clicking the button “>>”. Click on OK to close the window. Now click the search button to start the faceted search. A list of languages will now appear. Click on a language, such as “Spanish”. All persons will appear who have an aspect in which information is marked up as Spanish.
