Search filter

It is also possible to filter the results through a semantic classification. Perhaps you are looking for all persons that once stayed or lived in Rome. Choose “Persons” in the text field “Search For:” and choose “Aufenthalt” in the text field “Semantic”. Click on the search button. Now the results will only show those persons who have an aspect under the classification “Aufenthalt” and contain the word “Rome”.
