Perspectives and views

This chapter focuses on the display possibilities within the program, called perspectives and views. The former allows different “perspectives” of the data shown in the viewing area of the Archiv-Editor. For example, with one perspective you can display all the aspects of a person, and with another you can display overlapping data that potentially describe the same person. The main perspective plays here a central role. The two views–the aspect and tree-view–will be described with the help of examples and pictures as they are found in the main perspective. It should be noted however, that these views are used in the same way when opened in the two other perspectives (the identifier perspective and the concurrence perspective).
